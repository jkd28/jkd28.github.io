My Elevator Speech

My name is John Dott. I am a junior Computer Engineering student at the Univeristy of Pittsburgh who is looking to begin a career in Software Engineering. Following an internship at Prudential Financial in the summer of 2016, I started a new position as an Embedded Software Engineering Co-op at Zoll Lifevest. I will alternate semesters of work and school until the fall semester of 2018, when I am expected to graduate. I am a student member of IEEE and am always actively searching for ways to enhance my education both personally and professionally. In my free time, I act as president of the Pitt Krav Maga Club, where I instruct new students and improve my own knowledge of the Israeli combat system. I also spend my time reading, playing video games, and serving as a volunteer firefighter in my community.
